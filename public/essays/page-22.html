The Celebrity

Author: Youngjin Kang   Date: June 15, 2024

Look at this man of street wisdom,
He is the brightest star on Earth.

He steps out of every dirty matter,
And prides in his innocent smile.
He mirrors what everyone wants to see.

He preaches indolence with easy words,
And receives a shower of praise.
He echoes what everyone wants to hear.

Look at this man of plastic sugar,
He is everyone's friend.
