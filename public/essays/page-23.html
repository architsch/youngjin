A Modern Hero's Journey

Author: Youngjin Kang   Date: June 24, 2024

An average dragon kidnapped an average princess,
And took her to an average castle made of average diamond.

An average knight came to save an average princess,
With an average sword made by an average sorcerer.

An average knight slayed an average dragon,
And devoured an average chest hidden in an average room.

An average curtain came down an average stage,
And an average audience said that it was good.
