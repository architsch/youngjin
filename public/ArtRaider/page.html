ArtRaider

(This game is for Android only)

Virtual Art Museum

ArtRaider is an art museum simulator. Your goal is to steal artworks from virtual exhibition rooms.

Be an Art Smuggler

Collect money from stolen masterpieces. Be careful, however, not to get caught by the security officers!

Custom Map Editor

You can also design your own art museum, save it as an image file, and share it with other people!
