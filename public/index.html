About Me

My name is Youngjin, and I am a software engineer with a background in game development. I studied electrical and electronics engineering at the University of Washington, and worked for game companies including Signal Studios (Bothell, WA), Valkyrie Entertainment (Seattle, WA), and Galactic Entertainment (United Kingdom).

ThingsPool is a website I made as a personal project. It started as a simple blog, but it is nowadays undergoing a process of becoming a shared workspace.
