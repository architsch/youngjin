[An Elemental RTS]

Water vs Fire is a real-time elemental strategy game with minimalistic design.

<2>

[A Never-Ending Battle between Fire and Water]

You are fighting against hordes of deadly fire. Build wells to extend your water supply, and pour buckets of water to extinguish the menacing flames.

<3>

[This is a Race against Time!]

Extinguish every crumb of fire before the timer runs out.
