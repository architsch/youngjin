HuntLand

(This game is for PC only)

An HTML5 First-Person Shooter

HuntLand is a casual FPS action game in which you employ various tools to fight against your robotic enemies.

Emergent Mechanics

Make your way through bouncy projectiles, explosives, force fields, and other interactive wonders.

Roguelike Gameplay

Collect loot from the footsteps of your enemies, in order to level up and unlock your door to the next stage.
